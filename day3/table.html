About NEPAL SUPER LEAUGE:

The 2022 Nepal Super League is the second season of the Nepal Super League. Three new team will be added to the seven founding members. Due to a conflict between the teams of Martyr's Memorial A-Division League, Nepal Super League and Nepal Sports and Events Management the expected kickoff was postponed from 8th April until further notice. The season is expected to start in September.
